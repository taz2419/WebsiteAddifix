Ferrari 275 GTB / GTB/4

Focused selection of core restoration parts for the transaxle Gran Turismo

The Ferrari 275 GTB and GTB/4 pair Colombo V12 power with a rear transaxle, delivering exceptional balance and poise. Produced from 1964 to 1968, these cars combined elegant Scaglietti bodywork with advances such as independent rear suspension and a five-speed gearbox at the rear.

Our curated set of parts focuses on high-value components commonly required during concours-level restorations: braking hardware, driveline controls, instrumentation, and exterior trim. Each item is reproduced or finished to respect original geometry and finish, supporting faithful rebuilds of both two-cam and four-cam variants.
