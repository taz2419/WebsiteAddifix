Ferrari Dino 246 GT/GTS

Body panels and structural components for the mid-engined V6 masterpiece

The Ferrari Dino 246 GT and GTS, produced from 1969 to 1974, represented a revolutionary departure for Ferrari with its mid-mounted transverse V6 engine. Named in honor of Enzo Ferrari's son Alfredino, the Dino combined accessible performance with stunning Pininfarina styling and exceptional handling dynamics.

Our selection of Dino 246 parts focuses on critical body panels and structural components essential for authentic restorations. Each piece is precision-manufactured to match original specifications, supporting both GT coupe and GTS spider variants with proper fitment and finish quality for concours-level builds.
