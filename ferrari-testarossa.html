Ferrari Testarossa

Instrumentation, lighting, and interior components for the iconic flat-12 supercar

The Ferrari Testarossa, produced from 1984 through 1996 including the 512 TR, is defined by its wide stance, side strakes, and flat-12 performance. It represents an era of bold design and analog driving feel, with complex instrumentation and distinctive lighting and trim details.

Our selection focuses on key gauges, lighting, and interior hardware commonly required for faithful restorations. Each part is reproduced or restored to meet original fitment and finish standards for both early and later Testarossa models.
