Ferrari 348

Engine bay and instrumentation essentials for the wedge-era V8 berlinetta

The Ferrari 348, produced from 1989 to 1995, bridges the analog era with modern performance, featuring a longitudinal V8 and aggressive straked design. Its combination of mechanical character and Pininfarina styling makes restoration authenticity especially important.

Our curated parts list focuses on engine bay hardware, trim, and instrumentation required for faithful 348 restorations. Each item is selected for accurate fit and period-correct appearance.
