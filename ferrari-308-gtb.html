Ferrari 308 GTB/GTS

Exterior panels and trim pieces for the iconic V8 berlinetta and targa

The Ferrari 308 GTB and GTS, produced from 1975 through 1985, introduced a new generation of compact, mid-engined V8 performance. With Pininfarina styling, a high-revving four-cam V8, and timeless wedge proportions, the 308 remains one of Ferrari's most recognizable silhouettes.

Our curated list focuses on body panels, trim, and structural pieces most often required for faithful restorations. Every item is reproduced or restored to match original form and fit for both GTB berlinetta and GTS targa variants.
