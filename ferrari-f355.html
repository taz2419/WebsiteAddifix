Ferrari F355

Interior controls, lighting, and drivetrain details for the iconic V8 era

The Ferrari F355, produced from 1994 to 1999, refined the mid-engined V8 formula with advanced aerodynamics, a 5-valve engine, and one of the most evocative exhaust notes in Ferrari history. Its cockpit and instrumentation are equally iconic, making detail-accurate restorations essential.

Our selected parts focus on interior switches, lighting, shift hardware, and engine bay components most commonly required for faithful F355 restorations across Berlinetta, GTS, and Spider variants.
